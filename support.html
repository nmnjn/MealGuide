Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does MealGuide Premium work?
MealGuide Premium helps you take control of your health.

Based on the answers you provided in the app onboarding questionnaire, a nutritionist is assigned to you. You can then schedule a call with this nutritionist and explain all your needs to them.

Once they understand what you want, they will create the ideal diet plan for you. If you don’t like the plan, need changes or just need more clarifications and help, let them know by scheduling another call with them and and they’ll be happy to help!

2. How will MealGuide Premium benefit me?
With MealGuide Premium, you’ll get access to the following:

  • A dedicated nutritionist for all your needs
  • Personalized diet plans
  • Accountability for your health
  • Guided weight loss and health improvement
  • Full access to all recipes listed on the app
Premium helps you take control of your health.

3. What do I get on the free version?
In the free version you only get access to a limited number of recipes listed on the app.

With countless tasty recipes that you can make at home every day, filter out the recipes you like, add them to your weekly meal plan and start your healthy eating lifestyle today!
